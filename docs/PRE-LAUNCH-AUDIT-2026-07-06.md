**Date:** 2026-07-06
**Goal under audit:** paid marketing launch (TikTok / Instagram / YouTube) with hundreds of paying customers, a deliberately high paid/free ratio, technical robustness at 10K users, and a pedagogically excellent product.
**Method:** 7 parallel deep audits (Product/UX, Bugs/QA, Security/Privacy, Infrastructure/Scalability, AI quality, Monetization/Costs, Store/Marketing), each grounded in source with `file:line` evidence, each re-verifying the 2026-06-27 audit's findings against today's `main`. ## Executive summary

**Verdict: the product is good — much better than its economics, its measurability, and its store presence. Do not spend a euro on ads yet.** The core loop is polished and differentiated (persistent Lisa + cross-session memory is an ownable wedge no major competitor has), security posture is genuinely strong, and most of the June-27 P0s got fixed. But the audit found four launch-gating problem clusters:

1. **The unit economics are upside-down — by accident.** Two decisions you already made and spec'd — cheap TTS for free users, "Coach's voice = Pro" — were **never wired**. ElevenLabs (5–10× cost) serves everyone on the top-5 languages, so a _typical free user costs $4–10/month_ and even an average Pro subscriber is margin-negative. On top of that, the daily quota debit **trusts a client-reported number** (a modded client can debit 0 and burn unlimited minutes), and the RevenueCat webhook **strips Pro the moment a user disables auto-renew**.
2. **You are flying completely blind.** PostHog was never initialized (placeholder keys, zero events), there is no MMP / SKAdNetwork / TikTok / Meta pixel anywhere, the Deepgram rate cards under-report STT cost **10×**, the admin dashboard 500s, and there is no alerting of any kind (errors, uptime, or spend). Paid traffic today would be unmeasurable and un-optimizable.
3. **Two P0 data-corruption bugs shipped with continuous conversation (#82).** The stale-session guard still calls `/end` on the infinite thread (credits days of "practice" to streaks, sends the whole thread to OpenAI), and checkpoint duration counts the idle gap between sessions as practice time — every returning user's streak/stats are corrupted.
4. **The store pages ads would land on score ~29/100.** No iOS subtitle, Play category is literally "Tools", zero ratings, no video, English-only metadata in FR, and screenshots that render blank on weak connections (#28).

The good news: almost everything above is days, not weeks, of work — and the ~8 weeks until the natural early-September launch window is exactly enough to close all of it (see §8).

**Positive headline:** SecureStore migration done, RLS complete and correct on every table (incl. all new ones), no committed secrets, account deletion thorough and enumeration-safe, TTS fallback/idempotency engineering genuinely good, quota architecture sound, continuous conversation + barge-in + in-character error handling = real product quality.
