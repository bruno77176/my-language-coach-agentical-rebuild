# Pricing — My Language Coach

Last updated: 2026-06-08

My Language Coach is a freemium iOS and Android app for practicing spoken
conversation in 15 languages with an AI coach. Two plans:

## Free

- Price: $0 per month (no credit card, no trial)
- Conversation: 5 minutes per day, extendable by 3 minutes by watching a short ad
- Scenarios: 3 starter role-plays (ordering coffee, asking directions, small talk at a party)
- Feedback: current session plus the last 3 sessions
- Memory: basic, across sessions
- Platforms: iOS, Android

## Pro

- Price: $7.99 per month, or $49.99 per year (save 48%)
- Trial: 7-day free trial
- Conversation: 60 minutes per day
- Scenarios: all 10 role-plays
- Feedback: full history
- Memory: deep — weak areas, personal context, recurring themes
- Extras: weekly progress digest by email
- Platforms: iOS, Android

Upgrade happens inside the app after signup. Cancel anytime in your app store
settings.
